## Transportation Management Systems (TMS)

Platforms for planning, executing, and optimizing the movement of goods.

- [Oracle Transportation Management](https://www.oracle.com/scm/logistics/transportation-management/) — Cloud-based TMS for global logistics operations.
- [SAP Transportation Management](https://www.sap.com/products/scm/transportation-management.html) — Integrated TMS within SAP supply chain solutions.
- [MercuryGate](https://www.mercurygate.com/) — TMS platform for multi-modal transportation management.

## Freight & Shipping Platforms

Platforms connecting shippers, carriers, and freight services.

- [Flexport](https://www.flexport.com/) — Digital freight forwarding and global logistics platform.
- [Freightos](https://www.freightos.com/) — Online freight marketplace for comparing and booking shipments.
- [Uber Freight](https://www.uberfreight.com/) — Digital freight brokerage platform connecting carriers and shippers.

## Last-Mile Delivery

Technologies focused on optimizing final delivery to customers.

- [Onfleet](https://onfleet.com/) — Last-mile delivery management platform.
- [Bringg](https://www.bringg.com/) — Delivery orchestration platform for enterprises.
- [Routific](https://routific.com/) — Route optimization platform for delivery businesses.

## Fleet Management

Tools for managing vehicles, drivers, and operational efficiency.

- [Samsara](https://www.samsara.com/) — Fleet management and telematics platform.
- [Geotab](https://www.geotab.com/) — Fleet tracking and telematics solutions.
- [Verizon Connect](https://verizonconnect.com/) — Fleet management platform with GPS tracking.

## Tracking & Visibility

Platforms and technologies for tracking shipments and improving supply chain transparency.

- [Project44](https://www.project44.com/) — Real-time transportation visibility platform.
- [FourKites](https://www.fourkites.com/) — Supply chain visibility and tracking solution.
- [Shippeo](https://www.shippeo.com/) — Real-time shipment tracking and predictive ETA platform.

## Routing & Optimization

Tools for route planning, optimization, and logistics efficiency.

- [OptimoRoute](https://optimoroute.com/) — Route planning and optimization platform.
- [Route4Me](https://route4me.com/) — Routing software for delivery and logistics businesses.
- [GraphHopper](https://www.graphhopper.com/) — Routing engine and APIs for logistics applications.

## Warehousing & Fulfillment

Platforms and tools supporting warehouse operations and order fulfillment.

- [ShipBob](https://www.shipbob.com/) — Fulfillment platform for e-commerce logistics.
- [ShipStation](https://www.shipstation.com/) — Shipping and order management platform.
- [Flexe](https://www.flexe.com/) — On-demand warehousing and fulfillment network.
